Tmikha — tradition as support

We bring Jewish tradition into dialogue with psychology. Through a series of lectures and interactive classes, Tmikha offers reflective practices, language, and community to navigate inner life with steadiness.

  • Text & practice: teachings, case reflections, Q&A
  • Accessible explanations for all levels
  • Warm minimal setting — focus, not noise
